Court Tehsildar-Sadar, Varanasi. Case number-J202414700102510 year-2024 under section-34/35 U.P. Revenue Code, Mauza-Bajardiha Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil-Sadar District-Varanasi

Shri Rashid Jamal vs Shri Vijay Pratap Singh

Decision
This suit of transfer is recorded on the registered sale deed number-5717 dated-10/08/2023 received from the registration office Varanasi. Which was instituted on the basis of the transfer report of the Lekhpal. Arazi number-M.53 area 0.3040 hectare, area of ​​2720 sq. ft. i.e. area-253 sq. m., entire situated in Mauza-Bajradiha Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil and District-Varanasi has been sold by Shri Vijay Pratap Singh to Shri Rashid Jamal. As per rules, the suit was registered and proclamation letter was issued which was served and attached file is attached. The suit has been cancelled on 18-03-2024 in absence of original deed, affidavit and other evidence. The applicant has given an application for settlement dated 03-04-2024. Which is accepted. The unilateral order dated 18-03-2024 is cancelled and the suit is filed on the original number. In the absence of any objection, the suit is undisputed. The buyer has stated in his evidence that even after purchasing the said land, as per the original sale deed, extract Khatauni, affidavit, the buyer will have less than twelve and a half acres of land. The said land is not the land falling under Section 77 of the Gram Sabha and Revenue Code, the seller and the buyer do not belong to the Scheduled Caste. No adverse facts are mentioned in the transfer report. From the observation of the evidence available on the file, it appears legally appropriate to carry out the transfer proceedings in favour of the buyer.

Order Hence, in Mauza-Bajardiha, Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil and District-Varanasi, account number-00266, crop year 1427-1432, in Arazi number-M.53, area 0.3040 hectare, the purchased area is 2720 sq. ft., i.e. area-253 sq. meter, from the entire land revenue payee account, the name of the seller Shri Vijay Pratap Singh son of late Vishwanath Singh, address Flat No. G-1, Shubh Shrijan Residency, Lane No. 6, Niralanagar, Shivpurwan, Varanasi, should be rejected and the name of the buyer Shri Rashid Jamal son of Riyazuddin, late, address House No. N-12/366, Azad Nagar, Devpokhari, Bajardiha, city, Varanasi, should be mentioned as transferable landholder co-shared holder. After execution, the file should be filed in the office.

Satish Verma
Tehsildar-Sadar
Varanasi.
