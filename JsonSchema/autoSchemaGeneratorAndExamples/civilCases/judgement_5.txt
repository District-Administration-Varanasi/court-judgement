Court Tehsildar-Sadar, Varanasi. Case number-J202414700104425 year-2024 under section-34/35 UP Revenue Code, Mauza-Phulwaria Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil-Sadar District-Varanasi

Smt. Seema Singh vs. Shri Shriram

Decision
This suit of transfer is mentioned on the registered sale deed
No.-1040 dated-14/02/2024 received from the Registration Office Varanasi. Which was instituted on the basis of the transfer report of the Lekhpal. Out of the area number-693 of 0.1900 hectare, area of ​​2720 square feet i.e. area-252.78 square meters situated in Mauza-Phulwaria Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil and District-Varanasi has been sold by Shri Shriram to Mrs. Seema Singh. As per the rules, the suit was registered and a proclamation letter was issued which was served and the file is attached. In the absence of any objection, the case is undisputed.
The buyer has stated in his evidence that even after purchasing the said land, as per the original sale deed, extract Khatauni, affidavit, the buyer will have less than twelve and a half acres of land. The said land is not the land falling under Section 77 of the Gram Sabha and Revenue Code, the seller and the buyer do not belong to the Scheduled Caste. No adverse facts are mentioned in the transfer report. From the observation of the evidence available on the file, it appears legally appropriate to carry out the transfer proceedings in favour of the buyer.

Order Hence, out of 0.1900 hectare of Arazi number-693 of account number-00149 of crop year 1425-1430 F. of mouza-Phulwaria, Pargana-Dehat Amanat, tehsil and district-Varanasi, area 2720 sq. ft. i.e. area-252.78 sq. meter from the land revenue payat account, the name of seller Shri Shriram son of late Guddan, address village Phulwaria, Pargana Dehat Amanat, tehsil Sadar, district Varanasi, be cancelled and the name of buyer Smt. Seema Singh wife of Shri Santosh Kumar Singh, address Manasnagar Colony, Sector-B, Phulwaria, Varanasi, be mentioned as transferable landholder co-shared holder. After execution, the file should be filed in the office.

Satish Verma
Tehsildar-Sadar
Varanasi.
