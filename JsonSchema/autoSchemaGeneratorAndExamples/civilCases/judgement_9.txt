Court Tehsildar-Sadar, Varanasi. Case number-J202414700100022 year-2024 under section-34/35 UP Revenue Code, Mauza-Khanawan Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil-Sadar District-Varanasi

Smt. Sunita Singh vs. Shri Ramchandra

Decision
This suit of transfer is recorded on the registered sale deed number-9263 dated-22/12/2023 received from the registration office Varanasi. Which was instituted on the basis of the transfer report of the Lekhpal. Out of the area number-802 of 0.2800 hectares, area of ​​2736 square feet i.e. area-254.275 square meters situated in Mauza-Khanawan Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil and District-Varanasi has been sold by Shri Ramchandra to Mrs. Sunita Singh. As per rules, the suit was registered and a proclamation letter was issued, which was served and the attached file is attached. The suit has been dismissed on 02-03-2024 in the absence of copy of deed, affidavit and other evidence. The applicant has given a request for settlement dated 03-04-2024. Which is accepted. The unilateral order dated 02-03-2024 is cancelled and the suit is filed on the original number. In the absence of any objection, the suit is undisputed.

The buyer has stated in his evidence that even after purchasing the said land, the buyer will have less than twelve and a half acres of land as per the copy of sale deed, extract Khatauni, affidavit. The said land is not the land falling under Section 77 of the Gram Sabha and Revenue Code, the seller and the buyer are not from the Scheduled Caste. No adverse fact is mentioned in the transfer report. From the observation of the evidence available on the file, it appears legally consistent to carry out the proceedings of transfer in favor of the buyer.

Order Hence, out of 0.2800 hectare of Arazi number-802 of account number-00505 of crop year 1428-1433 F. of Mauza-Khanawaan, Tehsil and District-Varanasi, area 2736 sq. ft. i.e. area-254.275 sq. meter from the land revenue parta account, the name of the seller Shri Ramchandra alias Ramchand Rai son Kanhaiya alias Kanhaiya Lal Rai resident of village Khanawaan (Khanaon) Pargana Dehat Amanat Tehsil Sadar District Varanasi should be cancelled and the name of the buyer Smt. Sunita Singh wife of Ashish Kumar Patel resident of village Talar post Khairpur Semra Rajgarh Mirzapur should be mentioned as transferable landholder co-shared holder. After the execution, the file should be filed in the office.

Satish Verma
Tehsildar-Sadar
Varanasi.
