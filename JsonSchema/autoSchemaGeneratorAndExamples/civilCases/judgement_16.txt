Court Tehsildar-Sadar, Varanasi. Case number-J202414700103413 year-2024 under section-34/35 UP Revenue Code, Mauza-Keshripur Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil-Sadar District-Varanasi

Smt. Sushila Verma vs. Shri Lal Bahadur

Decision
This suit of transfer is recorded on the registered sale deed
No.-687 dated-01/02/2024 received from the registration office Varanasi. Which was instituted on the basis of the transfer report of the Lekhpal. Mr. Lal Bahadur has sold the area of ​​1142.4 square feet i.e. area-106.17 square meters situated in Mauza-Keshripur Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil and District-Varanasi, Arazi number-717/2 area 0.1700 hectare out of his legal share, to Mrs. Sushila Verma. As per rules, the suit was registered and a proclamation letter was issued which was served and the file is attached. In the absence of any objection, the suit is undisputed.
The buyer has stated in his evidence that even after purchasing the said land, as per the original sale deed, extract Khatauni, affidavit, the buyer will have less than twelve and a half acres of land. The said land is not the land falling under section 77 of the Gram Sabha and Revenue Code, the seller and the buyer do not belong to the Scheduled Caste. No adverse fact is mentioned in the transfer report. From the observation of the evidence available on the file, it appears legally appropriate to carry out the transfer proceedings in favour of the buyer.

Order Hence, of Mauza-Keshripur Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil and District-Varanasi, account number-00217, crop year 1427-1432 F., Arazi number-717/2, area 0.1700 hectare, out of its statutory share, area 1142.4 sq. ft. i.e. area-106.17 sq. meter, from the land revenue payat account, the name of the seller Shri Lal Bahadur son of late Mahadev, address resident of village Keshripur Pargana Dehat Amanat Tehsil Sadar District Varanasi, be cancelled and the name of the buyer Smt. Sushila Verma, wife of Shri Rakesh Kumar Singh, resident of village Badhaini Khurd, Post Babhanianv, District Varanasi, be mentioned as transferable landholder co-sharedar. After the execution, the file should be filed in the office.

Satish Verma
Tehsildar-Sadar
Varanasi.
