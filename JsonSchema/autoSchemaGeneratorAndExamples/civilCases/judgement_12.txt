Court Tehsildar-Sadar, Varanasi. Case number-J202414700102789 year-2024 under section-34/35 UP Revenue Code, Mauza-Kadipur Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil-Sadar District-Varanasi

Smt. Anita Devi vs. Smt. Sunita Sahu

Decision
This suit of transfer is recorded on the registered sale deed number-466 dated-24/01/2024 received from the registration office Varanasi. Which was instituted on the basis of the transfer report of the Lekhpal. Arazi number-782 out of the total area of ​​0.0050 hectare, area of ​​544 sq. ft. i.e. area-50.56 sq. m. situated in Mauza-Kadipur Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil and District-Varanasi has been sold by Mrs. Sunita Sahu to Mrs. Anita Devi. As per the rules, the suit was registered and promulgation letter was issued which was served and the file is attached. In the absence of any objection, the suit is undisputed.

The purchaser has stated in his evidence that even after purchasing the said land, the purchaser will have less than twelve and a half acres of land. The said land is not the land falling under section-77 of the Gram Sabha and Revenue Code, the seller and the purchaser do not belong to the Scheduled Caste. No adverse fact is mentioned in the transfer report. From the observation of the evidence available on the file, it appears legally appropriate to carry out the transfer proceedings in favour of the purchaser.

Order Hence, in Mauza-Kadipur, Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil and District-Varanasi, account number-00514, crop year 1425-1430, out of total area of ​​0.0050 hectare, area of ​​Arazi number-782 is 544 sq. ft. i.e. area-50.56 sq. meter from the land revenue pay account, the name of seller Smt. Sunita Sahu alias Sunita Devi, wife of Shri Sanjay Kumar Gupta, address house number B.34/6-3, Sarainandan Dashmi Ward, Bhelupur city, Varanasi, should be cancelled and the name of buyer Smt. Anita Devi, wife of Shri Jitendra Prasad Sahni, address village Ramchandipur Nakhwan, district Varanasi, should be mentioned as transferable landholder co-sharedar. After execution, the file should be filed in the office.
