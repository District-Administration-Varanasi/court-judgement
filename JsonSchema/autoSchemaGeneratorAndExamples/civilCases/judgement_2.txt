Court Tehsildar-Sadar, Varanasi. Case number-J202414700104274 year-2024 under section-34/35 UP Revenue Code, Mauza-Maduwadih Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil-Sadar District-Varanasi

Shri Shashank Shekhar Singh vs. Smt. Sudha Devi

Decision
This suit of transfer is recorded on the registered sale deed No.-914 dated-07/02/2024 received from the registration office Varanasi. Which was instituted on the basis of the transfer report of the Lekhpal. Out of the area of ​​0.4400 hectares, land number-M50, area of ​​2646 square feet i.e. area-245.91 square meters situated in Mauza-Maduwadih Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil and District-Varanasi has been sold by Mrs. Sudha Devi to Mr. Shashank Shekhar Singh. As per rules, the suit was registered and a proclamation letter was issued which was served and the file is attached. In the absence of any objection, the suit is undisputed.

As per the copy of the sale deed, extract of Khatauni, affidavit in his evidence, even after purchasing the said land, the buyer will have less than twelve and a half acres of land. The said land is not the land falling under section 77 of the Gram Sabha and Revenue Code, the seller and the buyer do not belong to the Scheduled Caste. No adverse fact is mentioned in the transfer report. From the observation of the evidence available on the file, it appears legally appropriate to carry out the transfer proceedings in favour of the buyer.

Order Hence, out of 0.4400 hectare of land parcel number- Mi50 of account number- 00398 of crop year 1430-1435 F of Mauza-Maduwadih, Tehsil and District-Varanasi, area 2646 sq. ft. i.e. area-245.91 sq. meter from the land revenue account, the name of seller Smt. Sudha Devi alias Sudha Sharma wife of Shri Premchand Sharma, address room number 443A D.L.W. city Varanasi, be rejected and the name of buyer Shri Shashank Shekhar Singh son of Shri Rakesh Pratap Singh, address house number B-38/47 B-K first floor Shri Hanuman Ji Society Motijheel Mahmoorganj city Varanasi, be mentioned as transferable landholder co-shared holder. After the execution, the file should be filed in the office.

Satish Verma
Tehsildar-Sadar
