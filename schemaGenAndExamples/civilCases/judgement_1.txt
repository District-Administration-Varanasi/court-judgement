Court Tehsildar-Sadar, Varanasi. Case number-J202414700104931 year-2024 under section-34/35 UP Revenue Code, Mauza-Bhagwanpur Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil-Sadar District-Varanasi

Smt. Madhuri Devi vs. Smt. Sita Devi

Decision
This suit of transfer is recorded on the registered sale deed number-1133 dated-16/02/2024 received from the registration office Varanasi. Which was instituted on the basis of the transfer report of the Lekhpal. Out of the area of ​​0.3020 hectare of Arazi number-529mi, area of ​​690 square feet i.e. area-64.12 square meters situated in Mauza-Bhagwanpur Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil and District-Varanasi has been sold by Smt. Sita Devi to Mrs. Madhuri Devi. As per rules, the suit was registered and a proclamation letter was issued which was served and the file is attached. In the absence of any objection, the suit is undisputed.
The buyer has stated in his evidence that even after purchasing the said land, as per the original sale deed, extract Khatauni, affidavit, the buyer will have less than twelve and a half acres of land. The said land is not the land falling under section 77 of the Gram Sabha and Revenue Code, the seller and the buyer do not belong to the Scheduled Caste. No adverse fact is mentioned in the transfer report. From the observation of the evidence available on the file, it appears legally appropriate to carry out the transfer proceedings in favour of the buyer.

Order Hence, in Mauza-Bhagwanpur Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil and District-Varanasi, account number-00907, crop year 1427-1432 F., out of Arazi number-529mi area of ​​0.3020 hectare, area of ​​690 sq. ft. i.e. area-64.12 sq. meter from the land revenue pay account, the name of seller Smt. Sita Devi, wife of Shri Raj Narayan Singh, address village Ishull, Tehsil Chakia, District Chandauli and present address Patel Nagar Colony, Bhagwanpur, Tehsil and District Varanasi, be cancelled and the name of buyer Smt. Madhuri Devi, wife of Shri Vansh Narayan Singh, address village Ishull, Post Pachwania, Tehsil Chakia, District Chandauli, be mentioned as transferable landholder co-sharedar. After execution, the file should be filed in the office.

Satish Verma
Tehsildar-Sadar
