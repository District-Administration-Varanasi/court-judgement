Court of Tehsildar-Sadar, Varanasi. Case number-202414700104593 year-2024 under section-34/35 UP Revenue Code, Mauza-Sarayanandan Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil-Sadar District-Varanasi

Smt. Poonam Devi vs. Shri Naseem Shah

Decision
This suit of transfer is mentioned on the registered sale deed received from the registration office Varanasi

No.-1227 dated-15/02/2024. Which was instituted on the basis of the transfer report of the Lekhpal. Out of Arazi number-36 area of ​​0.2830 hectare, area of ​​550.8 square feet i.e. area-51.189 square meters, the entire legal portion situated in Mauza-Sarayanandan Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil and District-Varanasi has been sold by Shri Naseem Shah to Smt. Poonam Devi. As per rules, the suit was registered and a proclamation letter was issued which was served and the file is attached. In the absence of any objection, the suit is undisputed.
The buyer has stated in his evidence that even after purchasing the said land, as per the original sale deed, extract Khatauni, affidavit, the buyer will have less than twelve and a half acres of land. The said land is not the land falling under section 77 of the Gram Sabha and Revenue Code, the seller and the buyer do not belong to the Scheduled Caste. No adverse fact is mentioned in the transfer report. From the observation of the evidence available on the file, it appears legally appropriate to carry out the transfer proceedings in favour of the buyer.

Order Hence, out of the area of ​​0.2830 hectare of Arazi number-36 of Mauza-Sarainandan Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil and District-Varanasi, account number-01016, crop year 1427-1432, area of ​​550.8 sq. ft. i.e. area-51.189 sq. meter, entire statutory share from the land revenue payment account, the name of the seller Shri Naseem Shah alias Mohd. Naseem Shah son of late Mohammad Naeem Shah, address house number N. 13/31A-R-1 Saraisurjan Bajardiha Varanasi, should be rejected and the name of the buyer Smt. Poonam Devi wife of Mukund Lal Kasera, address house number N. 14/227A2 Sarainandan Khojwa Varanasi should be mentioned as transferable landholder co-shared owner. After the receipt, the file should be filed in the office.

Satish Verma
Tehsildar-Sadar
Varanasi.
