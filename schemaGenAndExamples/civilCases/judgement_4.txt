Court of Tehsildar-Sadar, Varanasi. Case number-202414700104414 year-2024 under section-34/35 of UP Revenue Code, Mauza-Phulwaria Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil-Sadar District-Varanasi

Shri Santosh Kumar Singh vs Shri Shriram

Decision
This suit of transfer is mentioned on the registered sale deed received from the registration office Varanasi

No.-1099 dated-14/02/2024. Which was instituted on the basis of the transfer report of the Lekhpal. Out of Arazi number-693 area of ​​0.1900 hectare, area of ​​1360 square feet i.e. area-126.39 square meters situated in Mauza-Phulwaria Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil and District-Varanasi has been sold by Shri Shriram to the impostor Shri Santosh Kumar Singh. As per rules, the suit was registered and a proclamation letter was issued which was served and the file is attached. In the absence of any objection, the suit is undisputed.
The buyer has stated in his evidence that even after purchasing the said land, as per the original sale deed, extract Khatauni, affidavit, the buyer will have less than twelve and a half acres of land. The said land is not the land falling under section 77 of the Gram Sabha and Revenue Code, the seller and the buyer do not belong to the Scheduled Caste. No adverse fact is mentioned in the transfer report. From the observation of the evidence available on the file, it appears legally appropriate to carry out the transfer proceedings in favour of the buyer.

Order Hence, in Mauza-Phulwaria Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil and District-Varanasi, account number-00149, crop year 1425-1430, out of Arazi number-693 area of ​​0.1900 hectare, area 1360 sq. ft. i.e. area-126.39 sq. meter, from the land revenue payer account, the name of the seller Shri Shriram son of late Guddan, address village Phulwaria Pargana Dehat Amanat, Tehsil Sadar, District Varanasi, be cancelled and the name of the buyer Shri Santosh Kumar Singh son of Nathuni Singh, address Manasnagar Colony, Sector-B, Phulwaria, Varanasi, be mentioned as transferable landholder co-shared owner. After the receipt, the file should be filed in the office.

Satish Verma
Tehsildar-Sadar
Varanasi.
