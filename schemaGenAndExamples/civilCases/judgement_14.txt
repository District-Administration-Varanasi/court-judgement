Court of Tehsildar-Sadar, Varanasi. Case number-202414700105301 year-2024 under section-34/35 UP Revenue Code, Mauza-Nasirpur Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil-Sadar District-Varanasi

Smt. Tanya Pal vs. Mrs. Sushma Yadav

Decision
This suit of transfer is recorded on the registered sale deed number-1277 dated-20/02/2024 received from the registration office Varanasi. Which was instituted on the basis of the transfer report of the Lekhpal. Arazi number-Mi141/1 area 0.0490 hectare, out of the total purchased area of ​​1700 sq. ft., the area sold of 1360 sq. ft. i.e. area-126.394 sq. m. situated in Mauza-Nasirpur Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil and District-Varanasi has been sold by Mrs. Sushma Yadav to Mrs. Tanya Pal. As per the rules, the suit was registered and proclamation letter was issued which was served and the file is attached. In the absence of any objection, the suit is undisputed.
The purchaser has stated in his evidence that even after purchasing the said land, the purchaser will have less than twelve and a half acres of land. The said land is not the land falling under Section-77 of the Gram Sabha and Revenue Code, the seller and the purchaser do not belong to the Scheduled Caste. No adverse facts are mentioned in the transfer report. From the observation of evidence available on the file, it appears legally consistent with the procedure of transfer of name in favour of the buyer.

Order Therefore, out of the area of ​​0.0490 hectare of Arazi number-Mi141/1 of account number-00339 of crop year 1428-1433 F of Mauza-Nasirpur, Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil and District-Varanasi, out of the total purchased area of ​​1700 sq. ft., the sold area of ​​1360 sq. ft. i.e. area-126.394 sq. meter from the land revenue pay account, the name of seller Smt. Sushma Yadav, wife of Shri Shailesh Kumar Yadav, address 300 Narauli Dhanapur, District Chandauli, be dismissed and the name of buyer Smt. Tanya Pal, wife of Shri Amit Kant Yadav, address Urban Ecospace Flat No. A, 125, Village Adigarkallahalli, Near Exide Factory, Sarjapura, Bengaluru, Karnataka, be mentioned as transferable landholder co-shared holder. After the receipt of the document, the office should be entered.

Vaishnavi Construction vs Shri Sanjeev Kumar

Decision
This suit of transfer is mentioned on the registered sale deed number-1003 dated-07/02/2024 received from the registration office Varanasi. Which was instituted on the basis of the transfer report of the Lekhpal. Out of Arazi number-435 area of ​​0.4530 hectare, area of ​​4342 sq. ft. i.e. area-403.53 sq. m. situated in Mauza-Susuwahi Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil and District-Varanasi, Shri Sanjeev Kumar has sold it to Bahk Vaishnavi Construction. As per rules, the suit was registered and promulgation letter was issued which was served and the file is attached. In absence of any objection, the suit is undisputed.

The buyer has stated in his evidence that even after purchasing the said land, as per the original sale deed, extract Khatauni, affidavit, the buyer will have less than twelve and a half acres of land. The said land is not the land falling under section-77 of Gram Sabha and Revenue Code, the seller and the buyer do not belong to the Scheduled Caste. No adverse fact is mentioned in the transfer report. From the observation of the evidence available on the file, it appears legally appropriate to carry out the transfer proceedings in favour of the buyer.

After the execution, the file should be filed in the office.
