Court of Tehsildar-Sadar, Varanasi. Case number-J202414700102318 year-2024 under section-34/35 UP Revenue Code, Mauza-Lohta Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil-Sadar District-Varanasi

Shri Jalaluddin vs Shri Usman

Decision
This suit of transfer is recorded on the registered sale deed number-181
dated-10/01/2024 received from the registration office Varanasi. Which was instituted on the basis of the transfer report of the Lekhpal. Out of Arazi number-170 area of ​​0.0970 hectare, area of ​​652.6 square feet i.e. area-60.64 square meters situated in Mauza-Lohta Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil and District-Varanasi has been sold by Shri Usman to Shri Jalaluddin. As per rules, the suit was registered and a proclamation letter was issued, which was served and the attached file is attached. The suit has been dismissed on 18-03-2024 in the absence of original deed, affidavit and other evidence. The applicant has given a request for settlement dated 04-04-2024. Which is accepted. The ex parte order dated 18-03-2024 is cancelled and the suit is filed on the original number. In the absence of any objection, the suit is undisputed.
The buyer has stated in his evidence that even after purchasing the said land, the buyer will have less than twelve and a half acres of land as per the original sale deed, extract Khatauni, affidavit. The said land is not the land falling under Section 77 of the Gram Sabha and Revenue Code, the seller and the buyer do not belong to the Scheduled Caste. No adverse facts are mentioned in the transfer report. From the observation of the evidence available on the file, it appears to be legally appropriate to carry out the transfer proceedings in favour of the buyer.

Order Therefore, in the account number-00659 of Mauza-Lohta Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil and District-Varanasi, out of the area of ​​0.0970 hectare of Arazi number-170, area 652.6 sq. ft. i.e. area-60.64 sq.m., the name of the seller Shri Usman son of late Samad, address Lohta Alaval Pargana Dehat Amanat Tehsil Sadar District Varanasi, should be cancelled from the land revenue account and the name of the buyer Shri Jalaluddin son of Matin, address Lohta Alaval Ward No.-52 Pargana Dehat Amanat Tehsil Sadar District Varanasi should be mentioned as transferable landholder co-shared owner. After the execution of the file, the office should be filed.

Satish Verma
Tehsildar-Sadar
Varanasi
