
Court Tehsildar-Sadar, Varanasi. Case number-J202414700104026 year-2024 under section-34/35 UP Revenue Code, Mauza-Susuwahi Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil-Sadar District-Varanasi

Vaishnavi Construction vs Shri Sanjeev Kumar

Decision
This suit of transfer is mentioned on the registered sale deed number-1003 dated-07/02/2024 received from the registration office Varanasi. Which was instituted on the basis of the transfer report of the Lekhpal. Out of Arazi number-435 area of ​​0.4530 hectare, area of ​​4342 sq. ft. i.e. area-403.53 sq. m. situated in Mauza-Susuwahi Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil and District-Varanasi, Shri Sanjeev Kumar has sold it to Bahk Vaishnavi Construction. As per rules, the suit was registered and promulgation letter was issued which was served and the file is attached. In absence of any objection, the suit is undisputed.

The buyer has stated in his evidence that even after purchasing the said land, as per the original sale deed, extract Khatauni, affidavit, the buyer will have less than twelve and a half acres of land. The said land is not the land falling under section-77 of Gram Sabha and Revenue Code, the seller and the buyer do not belong to the Scheduled Caste. No adverse fact is mentioned in the transfer report. From the observation of the evidence available on the file, it appears legally appropriate to carry out the transfer proceedings in favour of the buyer.

Order Hence, out of 0.4530 hectare of Arazi number-435 of account number-00176 of crop year 1430-1435 F., area of ​​4342 sq. ft. i.e. area-403.53 sq. meter from the land revenue pay account, the name of the seller Shri Sanjeev Kumar son of Santosh Kumar, address plot number 119B, lane number 11, Ravindrapuri Colony, Bhelupur city, Varanasi, should be rejected and the name of the buyer through Vaishnavi Construction, proprietor Shri Vikalesh Kumar Singh son of Vijay Shankar Singh, address Sikhad Chunar, district Mirzapur, should be mentioned as transferable landholder co-shared holder. After the execution, the file should be filed in the office.

Satish Verma
Tehsildar-Sadar
Varanasi.
