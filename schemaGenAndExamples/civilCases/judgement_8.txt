Court of Tehsildar-Sadar, Varanasi. Case number-202314700117121 year-2023 under section-34/35 UP Revenue Code, Mauza-Sirgovardhanpur Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil-Sadar District-Varanasi

Shri Kashinath Pandey vs Shri Pramod Kumar Yadav

Decision
This suit of transfer is recorded on the registered sale deed number-3213 dated 19/05/2023 received from the registration office Varanasi. Which was instituted on the basis of the transfer report of the Lekhpal. Arazi number-1059/2mi out of area 0.0250 hectare, area of ​​2720 sq. ft. i.e. area-252.788 sq.m. entire portion situated in Mauza-Sirgovardhanpur Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil and District-Varanasi has been sold by Mr. Pramod Kumar Yadav to Mr. Kashinath Pandey. As per rules, the suit was registered and proclamation letter was issued which was served and attached file is attached. The suit has been cancelled on 15-07-2023 in the absence of original deed, affidavit and other evidence. The applicant has given an application for settlement dated 03-04-2024. Which is accepted. The unilateral order dated 15-07-2023 is cancelled and the suit is filed on the original number. In the absence of any objection, the suit is undisputed. The buyer has stated in his evidence that even after purchasing the said land, as per the original sale deed, extract Khatauni, affidavit, the buyer will have less than twelve and a half acres of land. The said land is not the land falling under Section 77 of the Gram Sabha and Revenue Code, the seller and the buyer do not belong to the Scheduled Caste. No adverse facts are mentioned in the transfer report. From the observation of the evidence available on the file, it appears legally appropriate to carry out the transfer proceedings in favour of the buyer.

Order Hence, in Mauza-Sirgovardhanpur Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil and District-Varanasi, account number-00472 of crop year 1430-1435 F. out of Arazi number-1059/2mi area of ​​0.0250 hectare, area 2720 sq. ft. i.e. area-252.788 sq. meter entire share from the land revenue payee account name of seller Shri Pramod Kumar Yadav son of Kanhaiya Lal Yadav address H.No. D 60/77 Choti Gaibi Keshavpur Police Station Laxsa Chhittupur City Varanasi be cancelled and name of buyer Shri Kashinath Pandey son of Vishwanath Pandey address village Pandey Pipra Post Sukal Pipra Mohaniya Kaimur Bihar be mentioned as transferable landholder co-shared holder. After execution of the receipt the file should be filed in the office.

Satish Verma
Tehsildar-Sadar
Varanasi.
