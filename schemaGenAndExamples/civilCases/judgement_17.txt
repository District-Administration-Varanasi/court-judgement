Court Tehsildar-Sadar, Varanasi. Case number-202314700135939 year-2023 under section-34/35 UP Revenue Code, Mauza-Kuruhua Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil-Sadar District-Varanasi

Smt. Rinki Agrahari vs. Shri Jit Narayan Patel

Decision
This suit of transfer is recorded on the registered sale deed number-8386
dated-09/11/2023 received from the registration office Varanasi. Which was instituted on the basis of the transfer report of the Lekhpal. Out of Arazi number-650 area of ​​0.2060 hectare, area of ​​686.25 square feet i.e. area-63.777 square meters situated in Mauza-Kuruhua Pargana-Dehat Amanat, Tehsil and District-Varanasi has been sold by Shri Jit Narayan Patel to Mrs. Rinki Agrahari. As per rules, the suit was registered and a proclamation letter was issued, which was served and the file is attached. The suit has been dismissed on 12-01-2024 in the absence of original deed, affidavit and other evidence. The applicant has given a request for settlement dated 06-04-2024. Which is accepted. The ex-parte order dated 12-01-2024 is cancelled and the suit is filed on the original number. In the absence of any objection, the suit is undisputed.

The buyer has stated in his evidence that even after purchasing the said land, as per the original sale deed, extract Khatauni, affidavit, the buyer will have less than twelve and a half acres of land. The said land is not the land falling under Section 77 of the Gram Sabha and Revenue Code, the seller and the buyer are not from the Scheduled Caste. No adverse facts are mentioned in the transfer report. From the observation of the evidence available on the file, it appears legally consistent to carry out the transfer proceedings in favor of the buyer.

Order Hence, out of Arazi number-650 area of ​​0.2060 hectare of account number-00099 of crop year 1429-1434 F of Mauza-Kuruhua, Tehsil and District-Varanasi, area 686.25 sq. ft. i.e. area-63.777 sq. meter from the land revenue payee account, the name of the seller Shri Jit Narayan Patel son of late Ram Lakhan, address Mauza Mudadev Tikri, Varanasi, should be rejected and the name of the buyer Smt. Rinki Agrahari wife of Vijay Agrahari, address H.No. N-2/136 HS, Mohalla Sundarpur, city Varanasi, should be mentioned as transferable landholder co-shared holder. After the execution of the receipt, the file should be filed in the office.

Satish Verma
Tehsildar-Sadar
Varanasi.
